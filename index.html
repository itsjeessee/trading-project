Strategy Overview
The Liquidity Edge
A discretionary price-action framework centered on institutional liquidity dynamics — identifying high-probability zones where large orders cluster, reading market structure shifts, supply and demand imbalances, and executing precisely at points of reaction. Pure price-based decision making — no indicators.

🎯

Entry Conditions

  • Price monitored continuously for taps or sweeps into pre-marked liquidity zones
  • Entry executed precisely at the zone upon price interaction
  • Focus on price behaviour and reaction at the level
  • High liquidity sessions only — London and New York
  • HTF trend alignment mandatory before any entry
🚪

Exit Conditions

  • Minimum 1:3 RR on every trade — below threshold skipped entirely
  • Target set at next liquidity pool or structural level
  • Trades held until target hit or stop reached
  • Extended holds when price showed continuation — patience rewarded
  • Partial exits taken on select high-RR trades
🧩

Setups Used

  • Fair Value Gaps (FVG) and Inverse FVG
  • Order Blocks — bullish and bearish
  • Liquidity sweeps — buy side and sell side
  • Trendline liquidity breaks
  • Accumulation · Manipulation · Distribution phases
🔍

Filters Applied

  • HTF trend alignment on Daily chart before any entry
  • High impact news avoided via Forex Factory calendar
  • DXY and crypto market cap monitored for directional bias
  • Ranging or low-liquidity sessions skipped entirely
  • BTC dominance tracked for crypto sentiment

Risk Management
The Core Framework
Stay Consistent With Risk Management — Personal Research
⚖️

1% Fixed Risk Per Trade

Every trade risked exactly 1% of capital regardless of conviction. This mathematical discipline ensures no single loss causes emotional or financial damage.

🎯

Minimum 1:3 RR Gate

Any setup not offering at least 1:3 RR was skipped entirely. This filter eliminated low-quality trades and significantly improved overall expectancy.

🏁

Monthly Target — Then Stop

On funded accounts, once the monthly target was achieved the account was rested.

Psychology & Discipline
What the Journey Taught
⏳

Patience is the Real Edge

Waiting for the right setup is harder than executing the trade. The strategy performs at full efficiency only in London and New York sessions — where institutional volume ensures clean, fast price delivery. Low-liquidity sessions avoided entirely.

🧠

Process Over Outcome

Strict rule adherence and periodic journal reviews built a process-first mindset. Evaluating trades on execution quality — not just P&L — reinforced long-term discipline and eliminated emotional decision-making.

🏆

Prop Firms as a Discipline School

Failing multiple prop firm challenges before getting funded was not failure — it was the education. Each attempt revealed a gap. Passing, getting funded, and receiving a verified payout proved the strategy and mindset had reached a professional standard.

🏛️

Proprietary Trading Firm — Funded & Verified

Strategy applied on a proprietary trading firm funded account — operating on simulated capital structured to replicate real market conditions. While the capital is simulated, the psychological pressure is entirely real: strict daily loss limits, maximum drawdown rules, and consistency requirements must be met to retain funding and qualify for payouts. Monthly target set and respected — once hit, trading stopped for the month. Payouts received are real cash disbursements based on simulated account performance.
